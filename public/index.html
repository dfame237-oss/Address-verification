How It Works: The 5-Stage Verification Flow

We use a proprietary, multi-stage engine combining Generative AI with official India Post data to guarantee the highest possible deliverability score.

  • 1.
    Generative AI Cleaning (NLP): The AI performs natural language processing, correcting phonetic and spelling errors, standardizing abbreviations ('rd' to 'Road'), and parsing the address into component fields.
  • 2.
    India Post Grounding: The PIN is validated instantly against the official India Post API to confirm the **District, State, and Official Post Office (P.O.) Name**. If the PIN is wrong, the AI corrects or suggests the deliverable one.
  • 3.
    Directional Landmark Prefix: We analyze the original text for key directional phrases ('near', 'opposite') and re-attach them precisely to the clean landmark field (e.g., "Near Apollo Hospital").
  • 4.
    Geo-Coding Confidence Check: The cleaned, grounded address is checked against proprietary geo-spatial data to ensure the P.O. and street level components are logically consistent.
  • 5.
    Deliverability Scoring: The final output receives a clear score (Very Good, Medium, Bad) based on component completeness, official validation, and geo-confidence, allowing you to manage risk.
